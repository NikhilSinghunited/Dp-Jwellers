Timeline

1941

The year D.P. Jewellers came into existence

The Foundation Stone As they say, “Journey of a thousand miles begins with a small step!” It was in the late 1940s when Late Shri DhoolChand Ji Kataria, dared to take that small yet revolutionary step. In an era when India was yet to become an independent country, DhoolChand Ji’s ambitious vision gave birth to a jewellery brand that transformed the ornaments industry in India forever. In the then highly unorganized markets his groundbreaking move of making 96% & 92% purity jewellery only led to a paradigm shift in the business!
